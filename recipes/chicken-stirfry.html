Tamagoyaki


Description

You can kinda tell what it is from the dish's name. Anyways, it's chicken that's stir fried with veggies.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup Soy Sauce
  • 1/4 cup Brown Sugar
  • 1 tbsp Cornstarch
  • Minced Garlic
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • 3 Chicken Breast Halves
  • Chunks of 1 Onion
  • 1 Broccoli Head
  • Sliced Carrots
  • High smoking point oil (e.g. Canola, Grapeseed, Peanut, etc.)

Steps

  1. Combine soy sauce, brown sugar, and cornstarch in a bowl, and stir until smooth.
  2. Stir in ginger, garlic, and red pepper flakes, then add the chicken and stir to coat.
  3. Cover and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 15 minutes.
  4. Heat up the pan on the highest setting until you see small streaks of smoke.
  5. When you see the small streaks of smoke, add some oil to the pan and swirl it around to oil up the pan's inner surface and wait for light smoke.
  6. Once you see the oil smoking, discard it to somewhere like, a ceramic bowl for later use to adjust moistness for later.
  7. Add broccoli, onion, carrots, water chestnuts, and bell pepper, then cook and stir until it's tender.
  8. Set the current contents of the pan outside to cook the chicken in about 2 minutes for each side or until it's browned.
  9. Add the previous contents back with the chicken's marinade and bring it to a boil.
  10. Cook for like 5-7 minutes more, or until you can't get any salmonella when eating the chicken.
  11. Serve.
