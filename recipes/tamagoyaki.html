Tamagoyaki


Description

Literally meaning "grilled egg", tamagoyaki is an egg dish with thin layers of egg rolled over to itself to form a log of sorts

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 tsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Mirin

Steps

  1. Add all of the ingredients into a bowl and whisk.
  2. Heat up a pan to medium high heat, then add a small amount of oil.
  3. Apply the oil around the pan using a paper towel or something similar.
  4. Add enough egg to cover the pan with a bit of leftover liquid.
  5. Once solidified, use chopsticks or any tool of choice to roll the egg.
  6. Re-apply some oil to the pan using the paper towel from earlier or something similar.
  7. Add some egg at where the roll stopped, then roll when solidified.
  8. Repeat until all of the egg mixture is used.
  9. Serve.
